# Foresight — Sales Rep Quick Reference

> Ask Claude anything below in plain English. Replace [ ] with real names.

---

## 📅 Start of Week

```
Pull my pipeline synopsis for [Your Name]
```
Your full week: scheduled calls from Salesforce calendar, top priority leads ranked by likelihood to buy, stale opportunities with coaching prompts, and leads that need research.

---

## 🔍 Research a Prospect

```
Research [Practice Name] in [City, State]
```
Full market analysis: population data, competitive landscape, SEO gaps, reputation analysis, Google Maps visibility, and a complete sales enablement summary with talking points, discovery questions, and objection responses.

After research completes — a **Gamma prospect deck is automatically generated** and saved to Salesforce. Share the link before or during the discovery call.

---

## 📋 Pre-Call on a Lead

```
Pull lead intelligence on [Lead Name or Practice Name]
```
Everything about a lead before you call: Pardot engagement score and grade, UTM source (how they found PDM), campaign attribution, research scores, competitive snapshot, recent activity, and a recommended call opener based on their primary gap.

---

## ⚔️ Competitive Pressure

```
Competitive alerts for [Lead or Practice Name]
```
Current status of their top competitor — review count, Google Ads activity, Maps pack position, pressure score.

---

## 🎯 Prospect Deck

After running research, Foresight automatically:
1. Generates a 10-slide Gamma prospect deck
2. Saves the URL to the Salesforce Lead
3. Creates a task in your Salesforce: "Your deck is ready"

Share the deck link via email before the discovery call.
Open it during the call — it's browser-based, no download needed.

---

## 📝 Log Activity

```
Log a call with [Lead Name] — [your notes]
```
Creates a Task in Salesforce instantly.

---

## 💡 Tips

- **Run research before every discovery call** — walk in with market data, not just a name
- **The pipeline synopsis pulls your Salesforce calendar** — make sure calls are logged as Events in SF
- **Likelihood to Buy score** tells you which leads to prioritize — focus on 60+ first
- **The deck is shareable immediately** — email it right after research completes
- **If a lead already exists in Salesforce**, research updates their record — no duplicate created

---

## Understanding Your Scores

| Score | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **Marketing Maturity 0-30** | No digital presence — blank slate, high upside, easy pitch |
| **Marketing Maturity 31-60** | Some presence, clear gaps — PDM fills specific holes |
| **Marketing Maturity 61+** | Established — harder sell, focus on competitive displacement |
| **Likelihood to Buy 70+** | Strong fit — prioritize, move fast |
| **Likelihood to Buy 40-69** | Good fit — needs nurturing |
| **Likelihood to Buy <40** | Lower priority — keep in pipeline but don't over-invest |
