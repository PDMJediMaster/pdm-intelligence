# Foresight — Account Manager Quick Reference

> Ask Claude anything below in plain English. Replace [ ] with real names.

---

## 📅 Start of Week

```
Pull my weekly brief
```
Your full week: scheduled calls, health alerts, renewal countdowns, doctor contact gaps, flagged accounts.

---

## 📞 Before Every Call

```
Pre-call brief for [Client Name]
```
Everything you need: account health, recent call notes, open tickets, active services, business objectives, Zoom AI summaries, and talking points.

---

## 🔄 Renewal Prep

```
Pull renewal proof package for [Client Name]
```
Baseline maturity vs. today, competitive position change, health trajectory, call sentiment, talking points, and a Gamma renewal deck — auto-generated.

---

## 🚨 Churn Risk

```
Which of my accounts are at risk right now?
```
Ranked list of your highest-risk accounts with health scores, risk factors, and MRR at stake.

```
Health report for [Client Name]
```
Full health score breakdown — engagement, cases, renewal proximity.

---

## 📈 Upsell

```
Which of my accounts have upsell opportunities?
```
Gap analysis — services they don't have that match their profile.

---

## 📝 Log a Call

```
Log a call with [Client Name] — [paste your notes here]
```
Creates a completed Task in Salesforce instantly. No manual logging.

---

## 🔍 Competitive Intel

```
Competitive alerts for [Client Name]
```
Current competitive pressure — who's running ads, Maps pack positions, review velocity.

---

## 💡 Tips

- **Fuzzy search works** — "Coastal Smiles" finds "Coastal Smiles Dental Care"
- **Be specific** — "pre-call brief for Smith Dental in Tampa" is better than just "Smith Dental"
- **Renewal package generates a Gamma deck automatically** — share the link with the client before the call
- **Weekly brief runs every Monday morning** — make it your first 5 minutes
